Mai Tai

Ingredients:

  • 1oz light rum
  • 1oz gold rum
  • .5oz orgeat
  • .5oz orange Curacao
  • 1oz lime

Preparation:

  • Add all ingredients in a shaker
  • Add ice and shake until well chilled
  • Strain into rocks glass over ice
  • Garnish with spent lime shell (filled with dark rum, if desired) and a sprig of mint

Slippery When Wet

Ingredients:

  • 1 large strawberry
  • .75oz lemon juice
  • .5oz honey
  • 2oz gin
  • 1 tsp Greek yogurt

Preparation:

  • Add strawberry, lemon juice and honey to a shaker
  • Muddle until well combined
  • Add gin and yogurt
  • Add ice and shake until well chilled
  • Double-strain into a rocks glass over crushed ice
  • Garnish with a strawberry slice and freshly-ground black pepper

Kentucky Buck

Ingredients:

  • 2oz bourbon
  • .75oz lemon juice
  • .75oz ginger syrup *
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 strawberry
  • Club soda

Preparation:

  • In a shaker, muddle strawberry with ginger syrup
  • Add lemon juice, bourbon, bitters and ice
  • Shake until chilled
  • Double -strain into a collins glass over ice and top with club soda
  • Garnish with a lemon wheel

Whiskey Highball

Ingredients:

  • 1.5oz chilled whiskey
  • 3-4oz cold seltzer water

Preparation:

  • Stack several medium-large ice cubes in a collins glass
  • Pour whiskey into glass, avoiding top of ice
  • Pour soda water in same manner
  • Using bar spoon, slowly stir, moving up and down to incorporate ingredients

Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 Mint Leaves
  • 2oz white rum
  • 1oz lime juice
  • .5oz rich simple syrup
  • Club soda to top

Preparation:

  • Place mint leaves in collins glass
  • Muddle gently
  • Add rum, lime juice and syrup to glass
  • Fill ¾ of the glass with crushed ice
  • Swizzle, using a swizzle stick or bar spoon
  • Top with a splash of club soda
  • Top with a mound of crushed ice
  • Garnish with straw and mint sprig

Queens Park Swizzle

Ingredients:

  • 2oz demarera rum
  • 1oz simple syrup
  • 1 large sprig of mint
  • 4 dashes of Angostura bitters

Preparation:

  • In a collins or hurricane glass, gently muddle mint leaves with simple syrup
  • Add lime juice, rum and fill with crushed ice
  • Swizzle with a swizzle stick or bar spoon
  • Add additional crushed ice and dash Angostura bitters over the top
  • Garnish with a mint sprig
